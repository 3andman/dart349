A stylisctic modern animated experience.

Design: The site opens with a calming, minimalist layout that has emphasis on visuals without overwhelming users.

Overall it's a beautiful way to explore a museum', fitting UNESCO’s mission, and opening visitors up to the concept whe they may not have otherwise.
