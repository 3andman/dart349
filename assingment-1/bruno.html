Bruno's Portfolio

A brilliantly crafted adventure through a portfolio display.

Design: The site looks more like an open-world video game than a portfolio, with 3D environments and full physics based interactions rather than a simple menu like mine.

Usability: Controls with WASD are clever but definetely challenging for users who just want to read about the creator if there even are any.

Content: The site turns something mundane into an entertaining interactive experience, creative but maybe not that efficient, though with the right target audience it's a fantastic idea and execution.

Creativity: This portfolio is extremely interesting and imaginative, driving around to learn about the developer is absolutely a unique idea and shows off the spirit of the creator.

Overall it’s a beautiful and creative portfolio that definetely stands out, it's impressive and very memorable.
