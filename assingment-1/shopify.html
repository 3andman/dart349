Shopify Live Globe

An unbelievably entertaining interactive company recap.

Design: The interface uses bold, colourful visuals and dynamic moving elements to celebrate Shopify’s Black Friday/Cyber Monday data.

Usability: Interactive and playful. Playing pinball demo turning a basic set of data into an enjoyable experience. Though some of the fun details are hard to experience with a lack of explanation for controls .

Content: The experience blends boring commerce data with visual storytelling, but it feels more like a game than just data, which I find to be a genuis way to reach the general public.

Creativity: This is probably the most unconventional large company website you'll ever see, data displayed by an arcade machine, computer monitor and a spinnable globe, it's compelling for anyone who doesn't just care to see the numbers, and I'm sure the people who want to see them have other means to find them.

Overall it’s exciting and playful, especially if you enjoy interactive visuals, it feels tailored to the average viewer and not just finance geeks.
